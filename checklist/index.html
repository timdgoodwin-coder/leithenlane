Systems & Integration

5 items
  • Your e-commerce platform connects to your email tool with real-time data sync (no manual exports)
  • Customer purchase data flows automatically between all your platforms, not via CSV files
  • You have a single source of truth for inventory across every sales channel
  • If you run subscriptions, the platform is properly integrated with your store and email
  • You've documented every tool you use, what it costs, and what it actually does for the business

Customer Experience

5 items
  • You have automated welcome, post-purchase, and win-back email flows live and working
  • Your support team can see a customer's full order history without switching between tabs
  • You're collecting customer feedback systematically and actually doing something with it
  • Returns and exchanges follow a clear, documented process (not dealt with on the fly)
  • You know your NPS or CSAT score and it's trending in the right direction

Commercial & Profitability

5 items
  • You know your true unit economics per product (including shipping, packaging, and returns)
  • You can tell which marketing channels are actually profitable, not just driving top-line revenue
  • Your discount strategy is tested and measured against a baseline, not based on gut feel
  • You track AOV, purchase frequency, and customer lifetime value at least monthly
  • You have a pricing strategy document, not just prices you picked when you launched

Fulfilment & Logistics

5 items
  • Your fulfilment partner (3PL or in-house) has an SLA and you're tracking against it
  • You have a reorder point system so you don't run out of bestsellers (or overstock slow movers)
  • Your packaging is optimised for both cost and brand experience (not just one or the other)
  • Shipping rates and zones are configured correctly with no margin leaks on delivery
  • You have a contingency plan if your primary fulfilment partner goes down

Team & Processes

5 items
  • Key operational processes are documented somewhere other than inside someone's head
  • You have clear ownership for each major business function (not everything landing on one person)
  • Your team uses a shared task or project management tool consistently (not just Slack and hope)
  • You review operational KPIs weekly, not just revenue and ad spend
  • You have a 90-day operational roadmap, not just a marketing calendar
